Frequently Asked Questions

What is StatusPay247?

StatusPay247 is a peer-to-peer advertising platform where users post sponsored content (provided by us) on their WhatsApp status and earn money for each approved status that meets our criteria.

Who can become a Poster?

Anyone who:

  • Is 18 years or older
  • Has an active WhatsApp account
  • Receives at least 100 views per status consistently
How does it work?
  • You register on statuspay247.com
  • After verification, we send you ready-made ads
  • You post the content on your WhatsApp status
  • We track and verify your post views
  • If your post meets requirements, you earn ₹20 per approved status
Can I post my own content along with the ads?

Yes. You're free to post personal content on your status, as long as you do not claim our content is your own or do anything that could harm the reputation of StatusPay247.

How many ads will I receive per day?

The number varies based on client demand. Some days you may receive 1–2 ads; some days more. We only pay for the ads we assign, not for anything else you post.

What are the view requirements?

Each assigned status must receive at least 100 views within the 24-hour period it is live on WhatsApp. If it receives fewer than 100 views, it will not be counted or paid.

How is my status verified?

We use internal tracking and may occasionally request screenshots for proof. View counts are also verified randomly to ensure fairness.

What if I forget to post or post late?

You must post the assigned ad within the time window we provide. Failure to post, or posting incorrectly, may lead to payment being withheld for that assignment. Repeated violations can lead to termination.

Can I choose which ads to post?

You cannot choose which ads to post. However, if we assign you an ad and you are unable to post it for any reason, you can contact the admin and explain your reason. If your reason is valid, we will assign the ad to another person. If we find the reason is not valid and you still do not post the ad, you will receive a warning. If this scenario is repeated, your account will be terminated without payment. Please note that rejecting ads will slow down your earning process.

How do I receive payments?

Payments are processed via bank transfer or UPI after your status posts are verified and approved.

Is there a registration fee?

No. Registration is free. You only need to meet the eligibility criteria to join.

What happens if I stop posting?

If you stop posting assigned ads, you will not earn. Continued inactivity may lead to account suspension.

Can I refer others to join?

We do not have a referral program for posters. However, if a poster brings a new client (advertiser) to StatusPay247, we offer a 5% commission on the client's first campaign spend as a reward.

Are there any penalties for non-compliance?

Yes. Non-compliance with posting guidelines may result in payment withholding or account suspension.

How do I contact support?

You can reach us via email at support@statuspay247.com or WhatsApp at +91 9744483058.
