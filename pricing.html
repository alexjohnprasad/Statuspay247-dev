Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know my ad was actually posted?

We provide screenshot verification of every poster's status upload upon request.

Can I target specific demographics or locations?

Yes! We can match your ad with posters who have specific audience demographics. Contact us for custom targeting options.

What types of content work best?

Visual content with clear value propositions performs best. We're happy to provide design guidance for maximum impact.

How quickly can my campaign start?

Once payment is received, we can typically launch your campaign within 24-48 hours.

Are there any discounts for longer campaigns?

Yes! Our Growth plan offers a 10% discount for monthly campaigns. Our Premium plan offers a 5% discount for weekly campaigns and a 10% discount for monthly campaigns. The higher your plan tier and the longer you advertise, the more you save.

What is the minimum campaign duration?

Our minimum campaign duration is 1 day. You can choose daily, weekly, monthly, or custom durations based on your marketing needs.
